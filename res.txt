 One  of  the  most  important  characters  in  fiction  is  named  "Tarzan  of  the  jungles  ."  Tarzan  was  raised  by  a/an  ape  and  lives  in  the  african  jungle  in  the  heart  of  darkest  area  .  He  spends  most  of  his  time  eating  fruits  and  swinging  from  tree  to  tree  .  Whenever  he  gets  angry,  he  beats  on  his  chest  and  says,  "  awuuuu  !"  This  is  his  war  cry.  Tarzan  always  dresses  in  crude  shorts  made  from  the  skin  of  a/an  lion  and  his  best  friend  is  a/an  small  chimpanzee  named  Cheetah.  He  is  supposed  to  be  able  to  speak  to  elephants  and  baboons  .  In  the  movies,  Tarzan  is  played  by  Mulama  .
